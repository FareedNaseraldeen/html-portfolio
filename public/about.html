About Me

Fareed Naseraldeen.


T has always been my passion. I have been working in various branches of IT for over 15 years. My career includes a significant tenure with the government and a parallel role at LG before I started my own business.

Unfortunately, the war devastated everything—our lives, community, and dreams. Those five years were the hardest of my life. I struggled to keep my family safe, moving from place to place, often without enough money for basic needs.

Deciding to come to Germany was not easy, especially since I had to cross the border illegally. It was a harrowing journey that lasted a month, but I made it and arrived in Germany in December 2015. I learned the language and began working in April 2017. I also managed to bring my family (my wife and two children) to join me.

Since then, I have improved my skills by learning another programming language, QA management, and process design. Currently, I am learning HTML and JavaScript.

Now, everything is much better, and I am proud of what I have achieved so far. I have a good job and a wonderful family. However, one thing is still missing—my homeland.

I am not sharing this story to seek compassion. I only want to tell you that no matter how bad things are around you, there is hope somewhere, and you just have to find it.

Stay optimistic.
